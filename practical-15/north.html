north sikkim

Discover the breathtaking beauty of North Sikkim. Nestled in the lap of the mighty Himalayas, this enchanting region offers pristine landscapes, snow-capped peaks, and gushing waterfalls. From the serene Gurudongmar Lake to the awe-inspiring Yumthang Valley, North Sikkim is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Embark on an unforgettable journey and immerse yourself in the tranquility of this Himalayan gem.
