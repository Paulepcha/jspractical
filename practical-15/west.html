west sikkim

Discover the scenic wonders of West Sikkim. Nestled amidst the mighty Himalayas, this enchanting region captivates with its lush green valleys, cascading waterfalls, and mystical monasteries. Trek through picturesque trails, witness breathtaking views of Mt. Kanchenjunga, and immerse in the rich cultural heritage. Uncover a hidden gem, where nature and tranquility harmoniously converge.
